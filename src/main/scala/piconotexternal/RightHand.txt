inState goingNorth
    surroundedBy xE** then move N newState goingNorth
    surroundedBy *x** then move E newState goingEast
    surroundedBy NE** then move X newState goingWest 

inState goingEast
    surroundedBy *x*S then move E newState goingEast
    surroundedBy ***x then move S newState goingSouth
    surroundedBy *E*S then move X newState goingNorth 

inState goingSouth
    surroundedBy **Wx then move S newState goingSouth
    surroundedBy **x* then move W newState goingWest
    surroundedBy **WS then move X newState goingEast 

inState goingWest
    surroundedBy N*x* then move W newState goingWest
    surroundedBy x*** then move N newState goingNorth
    surroundedBy N*W* then move X newState goingSouth
